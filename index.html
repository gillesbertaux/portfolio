SIDE-PROJECTS

  • TubeList ➞

    Product Design

    Growth

    TubeList is a new way to explore and discover everyday new YouTube channels and support the ones you like. Think of it as a Product Hunt or a Reddit for YouTube channels. TubeList is also a cool way for youtubers to share their work and interact with their community. We built it with Vincent . Featured on Product Hunt.

  • DataParis.io ➞

    Growth

    DataParis is a representation of Paris public data through the metro network. We built it with my teamates Robin, Vincent and Rémi. The Case Study is on Medium. Featured on Rue89, we even had a radio interview!

  • Techobject ➞

    Product Design

    Growth

    Techobject provides consumer insights on IoT products through integrated polls. It was initially built by Vincent and Tom. I do Growth Strategy as well as Product Design on features like Techobject News.

  • French Open Data Portal ➞

    Product Management

    This project was for Etalab, the french prime minister task force in charge of deploying the current open data portal. After a lot of research, this is the final prototype we delivered with my teamates Robin, Vincent and Rémi.

  • IMDB Redesign ➞

    Design

    I made this IMDB redesign as a side-project with my teammate Rémi. See the Case Study on Medium. The project has been featured on Sidebar.io, Gizmodo & CSS Design Awards.

  • Projectionist.io ➞

    Product Design

    Growth

    Projectionist.io is a theme-based list of 5 curated must-watch movies in your inbox every month. You can see all the past campaigns here. Projectionist.io currently has 250 subscribers.
